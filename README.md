## **ğŸ” Key Insights (Summary)**

- Students struggle with **safety**, **cost**, and **lack of verified information**.  
- Many listings online are outdated or misleading.  
- Students want **clear photos, detailed facility information, and accurate pricing**.  
- Availability near universities is often limited and competitive.  
- There is high demand for **advanced filtering**, **real-time availability**, **reviews**, and **map-based search**.
